Programmes

Support built around how your child learns best

Every plan starts with an assessment, so therapy reflects your child's real strengths and needs — not a one-size template.

🧩

Early Intervention

Structured, play-based sessions for children ages 2–6 focused on communication and daily living skills.

💬

Speech & Language Therapy

One-to-one sessions to build expressive and receptive language at your child's own pace.

🖐️

Occupational Therapy

Sensory integration and fine-motor support in a calm, purpose-built therapy room.

🤝

Social Skills Groups

Small peer groups that practise turn-taking, friendship, and emotional regulation together.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent & Family Coaching

Practical strategies so the progress made in the centre carries through into everyday life at home.

🎒

School Readiness

Transition support that prepares children and their teachers for a confident start to school.

A spectrum of strengths, not a scale of deficits.

Our team designs every programme around what each child already does well, using their interests as the doorway into new skills.

Families are partners from day one — every step, every small win, shared together.
